# LeadLift – AI Sales Assistant for Salesforce

LeadLift is an AI-powered sales assistant built on Salesforce that helps sales teams understand their leads better, predict deal closures, and automate outreach. It transforms raw CRM data into actionable insights so sales teams can focus on the most valuable opportunities.

## Problem Statement
Sales teams often struggle to identify which leads are most likely to convert. Traditional CRM systems store data but do not always provide intelligent insights.

LeadLift solves this by integrating AI capabilities into Salesforce to analyze leads, provide predictions, and assist sales teams in prioritizing opportunities.

## Features
- AI-powered lead analysis
- Opportunity prediction
- Intelligent sales dashboard
- Automated email outreach
- Lead prioritization
- Real-time insights from CRM data

## Tech Stack
